🤖 AI Agents Overview

Our three specialized AI agents work together to protect your digital life

📧

Email Parser Agent

The "Reader"

Takes raw email content and extracts useful details for analysis.

Main Jobs:

  • Parse headers (From, To, Subject)
  • Extract URLs, attachments, and body text
  • Detect named entities (person, org, dates, etc.)
  • Store structured info in MongoDB
  • Send cleaned data to Risk Scorer Agent

Tech Used:

NLP (spaCy) IR (Vector Search) LLM (Ollama LLaMA3)
🎯

Risk Scorer Agent

The "Analyzer"

Receives structured data and predicts how risky the email is.

Main Jobs:

  • Analyze sender reputation
  • Check links and attachments
  • Detect phishing keywords
  • Compare content embeddings
  • Combine IR + LLM reasoning
  • Output risk score (0-100) and reasoning

Example Output:

87% phishing risk — suspicious domain 'paypa1.com' detected and urgent tone in message.
🚨

Alert Generator Agent

The "Communicator"

Explains and reports what happened in human-friendly terms.

Main Jobs:

  • Receive risk score + reasoning
  • Generate human-friendly alerts
  • Store alerts in database
  • Show notifications on dashboard
  • Send alerts via email/Slack/Teams

Example Alert:

"This email may be a phishing attempt impersonating your bank. It includes a fake login link and asks for credentials."
